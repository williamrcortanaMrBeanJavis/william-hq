Pick 5 today. Uncle Adam checks your form on video.

Plank — 30 seconds
Core. Hold your body straight like a board. Breathe.
⬜
Dead Bug — 10 each side
Core. Lie on back, opposite arm + leg extend. Keeps your spine safe.
⬜
Superman Hold — 20 seconds
Back. Lie face down, lift arms + legs off ground. Strengthens your back for the bag.
⬜
Bird Dog — 10 each side
Core + back. On all fours, extend opposite arm + leg. Balance and strength.
⬜
Squats — 15 reps
Legs. Stand, sit back like sitting in a chair, stand up. Builds leg strength.
⬜
Push-ups — 10 reps
Upper body. On knees is fine to start. Build up over time.
⬜
Glute Bridge — 15 reps
Back + glutes. Lie on back, feet flat, push hips up. Protects lower back.
⬜
Side Plank — 20 sec each
Obliques. On your side, lift hips. Stabilises your spine when carrying the bag.
⬜
Star Jumps — 20 reps
Cardio. Gets blood flowing. Good for ADHD focus.
⬜
Cat-Cow Stretch — 10 reps
Spine mobility. On all fours, arch up then dip down. Undoes the bag damage.

🏋️ FORCE USA G12

Dad's G12 is a BEAST. Smith machine + cables + pull-up bar. Uncle Adam checks your form on video.
DAY A — PUSH (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)
⬜
Smith Machine Bench Press — 3 x 10
Chest. Bar guided = safe. Start with just the bar. Push up, slow down.
⬜
Smith Machine Shoulder Press — 3 x 10
Shoulders. Sit on bench under the bar. Press overhead. Builds the V-shape.
⬜
Cable Chest Fly — 3 x 12
Chest. Cables at shoulder height. Squeeze arms together. Feels amazing.
⬜
Cable Tricep Pushdown — 3 x 12
Triceps. Cable at top. Push down, squeeze at bottom. Arms get bigger.
DAY B — PULL (Back, Biceps)
⬜
Lat Pulldown (Cable) — 3 x 10
Back. Wide grip bar, pull to chest. This is what makes your back wide.
⬜
Seated Cable Row — 3 x 10
Mid-back. Sit, pull handle to stomach. Squeeze shoulder blades. Builds the muscles that fix your posture.
⬜
Pull-Ups (or Assisted) — 3 x max
Back + arms. Can't do one yet? Use the lat pulldown until you can. Goal: 1 pull-up by end of month.
⬜
Cable Bicep Curls — 3 x 12
Biceps. Cable at bottom. Curl up, slow down. The arm builder.
DAY C — LEGS (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes)
⬜
Smith Machine Squat — 3 x 10
Legs + core. Bar on shoulders, squat deep. The king of exercises. Smith bar = safe.
⬜
Leg Press — 3 x 12
Quads + glutes. Feet shoulder-width on platform. Push. Builds Jiu-Jitsu power.
⬜
Smith Machine Romanian Deadlift — 3 x 10
Hamstrings + lower back. Bar in front, hinge at hips, keep legs slightly bent. Protects your back from the school bag.
⬜
Calf Raises (Smith Machine) — 3 x 15
Calves. Bar on shoulders, rise up on toes. Tennis and Jiu-Jitsu footwork.
Weekly Schedule:
🟢 Monday — Day A (Push)
🟡 Wednesday — Day B (Pull)
🔴 Saturday — Day C (Legs)
• Start with just the bar or lightest plates
• 3 sets of each, 60-90 sec rest between sets
• Record form on video — Uncle Adam reviews
